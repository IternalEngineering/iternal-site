Things people ask
before working with us.

Do we have to start with an agentic build, or can we start small?
Start small if that's what makes sense. Many of our best client relationships began with a simple chatbot or workflow automation. The point is to start with something real, see it work, and grow from there. The Discovery Workshop is specifically designed to help you figure out the right first step.
Which AI model should we be using: Claude or GPT-4?
It depends on the task. Claude tends to outperform on long-document reasoning, regulated-sector deployments, and anything requiring careful, safety-aware outputs. GPT-4o is often better for multimodal work, code generation, and use cases that benefit from OpenAI's tooling ecosystem. We'll recommend the right model for your specific use case, not the one we prefer.
We've been burned by AI projects that never shipped. How is Iternal different?
Every project we reference is live and in production. We don't showcase pilots. Our delivery model (Forge) is built around shipping fast and iterating in the real world, not spending months in design phases. We also scope everything with fixed pricing so you're never surprised by the cost of getting to live.
Can you work within our existing security and compliance requirements?
Yes, this is genuinely one of our strengths. We have direct experience deploying inside NHS infrastructure, local government security requirements, AWS-compliant environments, and public-sector pen-testing processes. Compliance isn't an afterthought for us. It's built into how we scope and build from day one.
What does a project typically cost?
It varies considerably by scope. A simple automation or chatbot typically runs from £5k–£15k. A full agentic build starts around £25k and scales with complexity. Enterprise strategy engagements are scoped individually. Everything is fixed-price. No day-rate surprises. The Discovery Workshop (£2,500) will give you precise costings before you commit to anything.
How quickly can you start?
We keep capacity available for new engagements each quarter. A Discovery Workshop can usually be booked within 1–2 weeks. Build engagements typically start 2–4 weeks after scoping is agreed. We'll be transparent about our availability from the first call.

If there's a fit,
we'll tell you quickly.

If there isn't, we'll tell you that too. Discovery calls are 30 minutes, focused and free. We usually have space for one or two new engagements each quarter.
